About Me

Living and studying in Montreal, Canada, Ilianna Faoro is an artist currently completing her studies in Interactive Media Arts at Dawson College. Throughout her studies, she has created many works in various genres, such as digital art, musical arrangements, computational media and digital as well as physical games. She has always had a passion for both computers and art, which has motivated her to create work inspired by the intersection between the two.
